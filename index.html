About JOIN

JOIN is a global umbrella organisation for Jain communities and organisations worldwide. Its mission is to unite, integrate, and empower Jain organisations, promoting collaboration, global visibility, mentorship, and knowledge sharing while respecting the independence of each member organisation.

Pain Points JOIN Solves

For the Jain Community Globally

  • Fragmented Identity – No unified global presence.
  • Limited Access to Resources – Small organisations lack support.
  • Missed Knowledge Sharing – Initiatives are not shared efficiently.
  • Professional & Personal Growth Gap – Youth and professionals lack structured mentorship.
  • Reduced Global Visibility – Community initiatives remain local.
  • Duplication of Efforts – Similar projects run independently.
  • Limited Collaboration Across Fields – Spiritual, business, education, health, and youth programs operate in silos.
  • Youth Engagement & Retention – Younger generation may not see value in isolated organisations.

For Jain Organisations (as Members)

  • Structured Networking – Access to global leaders, philanthropists, and experts.
  • Digital Ecosystem – Centralised portal/app for communication and events.
  • Shared Resources & Knowledge Hub – Training materials, research, and programs.
  • Global Advocacy – Strong representation at international forums.
  • Mentorship & Professional Programs – Guide youth and freshers in careers, education, and personal development.
  • Philanthropy Alignment – Efficient channeling of donations through a global fund.

Challenges of Jain Organisations Working in Isolation

  • Limited Reach & Impact – Only local/regional influence.
  • Resource Constraints – Staff, funding, and technical support are limited.
  • Duplication & Inefficiency – Running similar initiatives wastes effort.
  • Difficulty in Knowledge Sharing – Hard to learn from other organisations.
  • Limited Youth Engagement – Young members cannot easily connect.
  • Difficulty in Attracting Global Talent – Professionals abroad cannot contribute easily.
  • Fragmented Representation – Lack of unified voice globally.
  • Limited Visibility & Recognition – Achievements go unnoticed outside local circles.

Benefits of JOIN

Unified Global Voice

Stronger influence in policy, interfaith dialogues, and global decision-making.

Shared Resources

Access to funding, volunteers, training, research, and programs globally.

Youth & Leadership

Mentorship, career guidance, and programs to engage the next generation.

Collaboration, Not Competition

Organisations retain independence but align with global pillars.

Global Visibility

Showcase local initiatives through a unified digital platform and global events.

Philanthropy Impact

Efficient channelling of donations and resources through JOIN Global Fund.

Implementation Roadmap

Phase 1 – Foundation (0–6 months)

  • Form Core Committee of visionary leaders.
  • Define constitution, vision, and bylaws.
  • Build JOIN Digital Platform (website + app).
  • Start outreach to existing Jain organisations for partnerships.

Phase 2 – Expansion (6–18 months)

  • Launch regional chapters globally.
  • Set up pillar working groups.
  • Organise first Global Jain Summit.

Phase 3 – Institutionalisation (18–36 months)

  • Establish Global Jain Resource Center.
  • Create JOIN Global Fund for philanthropy.
  • Launch flagship programs (scholarships, youth leadership, business incubator, Jain UN Day).
